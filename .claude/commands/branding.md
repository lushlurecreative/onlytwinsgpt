# OnlyTwins Branding Context

You are about to work on UI, copy, or creative direction for **OnlyTwins** — a premium AI content generation platform. Before doing anything, internalise the following brand context completely.

---

## What OnlyTwins is

OnlyTwins is a high-end AI platform that lets creators generate professional-grade content of themselves using their own trained AI model. Consumers subscribe to receive personalised AI-generated content from creators they follow.

This is not a generic SaaS tool. It is a luxury digital product in a competitive, emotionally-charged space. Every pixel and every word must reflect that.

---

## Brand personality

| Trait | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| **Premium** | Never cheap, never cluttered. Think Apple, not Facebook. |
| **Confident** | No hedging. No "we hope you enjoy". State things plainly and boldly. |
| **Discreet** | Never clinical or blunt about what the product does. Sophisticated, tasteful language always. |
| **Modern / AI-native** | Leans into AI as a superpower, not a disclaimer. |
| **Exclusive** | Language implies access, membership, elevation — not mass-market. |

---

## Colours

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `#0a0a0a` | Page backgrounds — near-black |
| Surface | `#111111` | Cards, panels |
| Border | `#1f1f1f` | Subtle dividers |
| Accent / Primary | `#7c3aed` | Violet — CTAs, highlights, glow effects |
| Accent light | `#a78bfa` | Hover states, secondary emphasis |
| Text primary | `#f5f5f5` | Headings, key copy |
| Text secondary | `#9ca3af` | Supporting text, labels |
| Success | `#10b981` | Confirmations, completed states |
| Destructive | `#ef4444` | Errors, warnings |

Always dark mode. Never use white backgrounds in any customer-facing UI.

---

## Typography

- Headings: bold, tight tracking, large — command attention
- Body: clean, readable, never cramped
- CTAs: concise, active voice, one clear action per button
- No sentence-case inconsistency — headings are title case or all-caps for impact

---

## Copy rules

**Do:**
- Use active, direct language: "Generate your content", "Start creating", "Unlock your vault"
- Use exclusivity cues: "Your AI model", "Your monthly batch", "Private vault"
- Speak to aspiration: what the user *becomes*, not what the tool *does*
- Keep CTAs to 2–4 words

**Don't:**
- Say "Stripe", "RunPod", "Supabase", "webhook", or any internal technical term
- Use passive voice in CTAs ("Content will be generated" → "Generating your content")
- Use filler phrases: "Welcome to", "We're excited to", "Please note"
- Show raw IDs, status codes, or system errors to customers
- Use generic SaaS language: "Get started", "Sign up today", "Learn more"

---

## Target personas

**Creator** — Individual (typically 18–35) who wants to monetise their likeness via AI-generated content without constant manual effort. Values: control, privacy, premium quality, passive income.

**Consumer** — Fan or subscriber who pays for personalised AI content from creators they follow. Values: access, exclusivity, quality.

Both personas are sophisticated. Do not talk down to them.

---

## Visual style rules

- Glassmorphism, subtle gradients, dark surfaces
- Violet glow effects on key CTAs and hero elements
- No stock photography in UI — use brand assets only
- Animations: purposeful, smooth, not flashy — Framer Motion with short durations (0.2–0.4s)
- Icons: minimal, line-style (Lucide preferred)
- Spacing: generous — never cramped

---

## What to do now

Apply this brand context to all UI, copy, and design decisions in this session. If the task involves:

- **Writing copy** — match tone, avoid forbidden phrases, use persona-aware language
- **Building UI** — enforce colour tokens, dark surfaces, violet accents
- **Designing flows** — prioritise clarity, confidence, and premium feel at every step

Ask if you need clarification on which persona this feature targets before writing copy.
